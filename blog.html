The Evolution of Medical Gloves: From Latex to Advanced PU Technology

February 13, 2025

The evolution of medical gloves marks a significant shift from traditional latex to innovative polyurethane (PU) technology. While latex gloves served healthcare for nearly a century, they posed challenges including allergic reactions and limited durability. PU gloves represent a revolutionary advancement, offering enhanced protection, superior tactile sensitivity, and improved sustainability. Modern PU gloves feature micro-textured surfaces, antimicrobial properties, and advanced moisture management systems. The technology continues to evolve with developments in smart gloves and enhanced barrier properties. This transformation reflects a fundamental advancement in medical protection, promising continued innovation in healthcare safety and environmental responsibility.
